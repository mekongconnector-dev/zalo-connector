== Description ==
The Zalo Connect plugin is designed to enhance your e-commerce website by providing seamless integration with Zalo, one of the most popular social media and messaging platforms in Vietnam. 
With this plugin, your customers can easily log in to your online store using their Zalo accounts, making the registration and login process quick and convenient.

== Key Features ==
1. Zalo Login Integration: Allow customers to log in and register on your website using their Zalo accounts. Simplify the registration process and reduce friction for users.

2. Zalo Followers Sync: Effortlessly synchronize Zalo followers with your website's customer database. Keep your customer information up to date and improve your marketing efforts.

3. Customer Insights: Gain valuable insights into your customers' behavior. Track total sales and orders, allowing you to better understand your customer base and tailor your marketing strategies accordingly.

== Benefits ==
1. Enhanced User Experience: Offer a user-friendly and familiar login method through Zalo, reducing the need for customers to remember additional credentials.

2. Increased Customer Engagement: Leverage your Zalo followers for more effective marketing campaigns and promotions.

3. Data-Driven Decisions: Access valuable data about your customer base, allowing you to make informed business decisions and improve customer satisfaction.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
Q: How do I add the login button to my website?
A: Adding the Zalo login button to your website is a breeze. The plugin takes care of this automatically. Once you've configured your Zalo API credentials and settings, the login button will be seamlessly integrated into your website. No additional steps are required.

Q: Can I easily track the orders that customers have made?
A: Yes, you can effortlessly track the status of orders made by your customers. The Zalo Connect plugin provides you with a dedicated dashboard where you can view and manage all customer orders. This allows you to keep tabs on order status and manage your e-commerce activities effectively.

== Installation ==
1. Upload the "zalo-connector" folder to the "/wp-content/plugins/" directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

== Plugin Requirements ==
- WordPress version 4.6 or higher.
